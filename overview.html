The global standard for loyalty on the blockchain

Meta Study network will have a common token named MTS which will be used with all educators in the networks and allow them to issue their own rewarding token back by MTS to reward to users/students. Their students can redeem, swap, exchange the tokens in Meta Study network's exchange & common market place for NFT, products/services or join Meta Study network's events/challenges to earn more.

Our Loyalty Solution

Loyalty Protocol

Our loyal customers who benefit from having real value, usability and freedom.

Decentralized loyalty apps. The MetaStudy wallet is one of the means by which loyal customers can interact with the LoyaltyToken Protocol. The MetaStudy wallet directly communicates with our API which talks to the protocol.

Our SDK streamlines the loyalty token creation process for brands. As part of the SDK, the API is the main component and it acts as a bridge between a decentralized app and our protocol. It is also how brands can join the ecosystem and build and configure their own loyalty tokens.

The LoyaltyToken Protocol enables the communication between the public Binance Smart chain and the different loyalty blockchains to make it possible for brands to set up and run their loyalty programs.

Blockchain infrastructure. Our protocol is capable of communicating with both the public Binance Smart Chain and the loyalty blockchains. Together, these blockchains make up the blockchain infrastructure

Our Loyalty Solution

Loyalty Rewards Issuance

Our protocol provides a trustless, secure and provable manner for issuing loyalty tokens backed by a crypto asset across different blockchains. It also guarantees liquidity by allowing easy redemption, tradability and exit options.

The LoyaltyToken Protocol is made up of two fundamental components: one blockchain containing a crypto asset that backs the loyalty tokens with a real value, one private network where the loyalty tokens reside, a set of loyalty contracts that specify how these loyalty tokens are, behave and can be exchanged between them.

Design of token economics

Blockchain tech & crypto empowers educators

Earning rewarding tokens

Students can be more active in their learning thanks to the #study-to-earn mechanism provided by Meta Study networks. They can earn token by doing homeworks, achieve milestone or win an event/challenge/ competition.

By doing homeworks, activities in educators' platform, they can improve their skills,knowledge, abilities .. much better than before with passive study, less engagement.

Spending rewarding tokens

Users who earned tokens can spend in MetaStudy network's market place, which can swap for NFT, or MTS. Then can use MTS to buy product/ services from ALL participating partners (educator) of MetaStudy network.
